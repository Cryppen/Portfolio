Automating the Booking Process at Aalborg Zoo

Abstract

The communication management at Aalborg Zoo experience manually handling large amounts of data to be a reoccurring issue in their booking process for arrangements and lectures. Therefore, they requested a system, which could structure and automate the booking process in a user-friendly manner to achieve a greater overview and reduce inconsistencies. For the system, a list of requirements was agreed upon in cooperation with the communication management. To pursue the Object Oriented Analysis and Design standard, the system was developed using the Iterative model. The initial analyses focuses on the Problem Domain and Application Domain, which elaborates on the way the booking process is currently handled at the Zoo and afterwards determines the required functionality of the new system. This is followed by an elaboration on the Architecture-and Component design that provided guidance in the later implementation of the booking system. To test the quality of the system and as a part of the Iterative model, three usability tests have been conducted to evaluate on the functionality and the usability of the system. The system has thereafter been adjusted or expanded by additional requirements to achieve the most optimal booking system for the Zoo. By fulfilling the requirements from the initial system definition and the additional requirements from usability tests, the system is concluded as appropriate to solve the issue at Aalborg Zoo.

On an important note; While the software solution was tailored for their needs, the project was never used at Aalborg Zoo.

My Contributions

Since the project is a 7 person collaboration I feel it's fair to outline my contributions to the project. My major role in the implementation was regarding the communication between the google calendar API with OAuth2 login and confirmations. The system had to update their calendars around the office with change of arrangements and events. Besides the google calendar implementation my focus was also on the application and the search function of the bookings. In the application I did some of the statistics work of the applications creating graphs over bookings to track progress.
